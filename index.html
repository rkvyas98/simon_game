How to play this game

As the game starts,Simon will give the first signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color lens. Second time another signal will be generated, then you have to press the first button and then the second button in sequence. After you press first and second button successfully, third time another signal will be pressed and then you have to follow the order by pressing first, second and third button and this how the flow of game goes on. When you break the sequence of pressing buttons in correct order, your game will be over.
